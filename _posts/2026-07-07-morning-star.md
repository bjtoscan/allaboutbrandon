"Morning Star" is the third installment of the Red Rising trilogy. I was hesitant to start the trilogy at first, but a dear friend recommended it as one that would always leave me wanting more. The series, in my opinion, has been highlighted by character development and adaptability, shown through the actions taken by each character and their resulting consequences. I highly recommend such a phenomenal story for anyone willing to throw their fear of an invisible ceiling to the wayside as they follow Darrow and his companions to new heights.
