My name is Brandon Toscano. To get to know me, here are a couple fun facts about me.

- I was born and raised in San Diego (Go Padres!)
- I love learning because I can sharpen my skillset.
- I also love teaching because I believe it can help me learn while helping others.
- I like to be outside and enjoy golfing, biking, and going to the beach.

### My story

Growing up, I have always appreciated working with and for others. I noticed this when I began tutoring classmates and grew this passion in varioud ways from participating in my high school's tutoring program to flying across the country for a week-long service trip in Philadelphia. This was not a phase for me. Rather, it's who I am as a person.

## Program Evaluation and Data Analytics

My favorite subject in school never used to be math. I thought it was tedious and annoying for me to understand the interaction of numbers whose definitions are unknown. It was a classic case of "When will I ever use this?". However, I slowly realized how much numbers and data do for us in an attempt to quantify aspects of our life while assisting in making decisions. The realization now leads me to explore all the different ways that data can help improve the daily lives of others.
